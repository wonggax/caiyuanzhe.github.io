研发方向：致力于医疗耗材和医疗器械的研发，专注于提升医疗服务的质量和效率

Jimeng Sun is the Health Innovation Professor at Computer Science Department and Carle's Illinois College of Medicine at University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. Previously, he was at the College of Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology.

His research interest is on artificial intelligence (AI) for healthcare:

Deep learning for drug discovery, Clinical trial optimization, Computational phenotyping, Clinical predictive modeling, Treatment recommendation, Health monitoring.
